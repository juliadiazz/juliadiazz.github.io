Vacation

Characters

  • Clark - Chevy Chase
  • Ellen - Beverly D'Angelo
  • Russel - Anthony Michael Hall
  • Audrey - Dana Barron
Clark Griswold, wanting to spend more time with his wife Ellen and children Rusty and Audrey, decides to lead the family on a cross-country expedition from the Chicago suburbs to the southern California amusement park Walley World, during the family's travels, they experience numerous mishaps.
