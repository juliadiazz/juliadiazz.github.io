Annie is a single woman whose own life is a mess, but when she learns that her lifelong best friend, Lillian is engaged, she has no choice but to serve as the maid of honor. Determined to make things perfect, she gamely leads Lillian and the other bridesmaids down the wild road to the wedding.>
